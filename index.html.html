Our Story

A garden escape
in the city

Nestled in the leafy suburb of Klein Windhoek, Wilde Eend Bistro is Windhoek's beloved garden café — a place where the pace slows down, the coffee is always fresh, and breakfast stretches into afternoon.

Dine beneath towering camelthorn trees in our outdoor garden, where dappled light and open air make every meal an occasion. Whether you're popping in for a morning flat white or settling in for a long brunch with friends, there's always a table under the trees.
